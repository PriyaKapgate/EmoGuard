# EmoGuard: Mental Health Detection and Support

**EmoGuard** is an innovative mobile application designed to assess and support mental health through multiple advanced techniques, including the **PHQ-9 test**, **facial emotion recognition**, **audio emotion detection**, and AI-powered **chatbot support**. The app also provides **mental health exercises** and **emergency doctor support** to ensure users receive timely help.

## üöÄ Features

- **PHQ-9 Test**: A well-established, standardized questionnaire that assesses the severity of depression.
- **Facial Emotion Detection**: Real-time facial analysis to detect emotional states (e.g., sadness, joy, anger).
- **Audio Emotion Detection**: Analysis of the user's voice tone and speech patterns to identify emotional cues.
- **AI Chatbot**: Provides instant support, emotional coaching, and guidance based on user input.
- **Mental Health Exercises**: Access a variety of therapeutic exercises designed to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression.
- **Emergency Doctor Support**: In cases of mental health crises, the app can connect users to licensed emergency mental health professionals for immediate support.

## üå± **How It Works**

1. **PHQ-9 Test**: The app will prompt the user to take the PHQ-9 depression questionnaire, generating a score to assess the severity of their mental health.
2. **Emotion Recognition**: The app uses advanced algorithms for **facial emotion recognition** and **voice analysis** to detect emotional states based on real-time inputs.
3. **AI Chatbot**: The chatbot interacts with the user, offering emotional support, mindfulness exercises, and suggestions based on their responses and emotional states.
4. **Mental Health Exercises**: The app offers personalized exercises based on the user's emotional state, aiming to alleviate stress, depression, and anxiety.
5. **Emergency Support**: If the app detects signs of severe mental distress, it can connect the user with an emergency doctor or mental health professional.
